The Goal of this Project is to Provide an All-Access, Open-Sourced, Interactive Encyclopedia.

We Believe the future of the Encyclopedia should embody the connected nature of Knowledge and by doing so, construct a visually intuitive User-Experience.

All of the web technologies necessary to make this possible are already built in some form or another, as individual components, they're just scattered around a multitude of different popular and less-well-known resources on the web.

Most of these venues are already-useful web applications ranging from Wikipedia, WolframAlpha, Google, Academic Databases, Major Online News Organizations, Reddit/Blogs, and several data-vizualization services.

Help Create the Evolution of How We Access and Understand Human Knowledge...
