Project-Consilience > Vizupedia.org

   The Insight of Data Visualization meets the Power of Interactivity... Vizupedia, the Encylopedia that embodies human knowledge in its true nature: an interconnected, nonlinear and decentralized network.

   Unlock perspective through iconography; Dissect the fractured branching of various subject matters by browsing the network and clicking to either reveal a node's sub-topics or show connections with other related subjects. Explore endless levels in the hierarchy of disciplines while maintaining a synoptic overview of the whole network of knowledge

  Search Vizupedia for any specific topic you'd like to know more about, and watch as the network animates and unwraps to reveal the contextual location of your query.

   View any node's encyclopedic article and factual data at any time whether navigating through nodes or inquiring about an explicit subject. All nodes have the option to reveal detailed source information, so you can learn in confidence with the benefit of extending your research to external sites and organizations.

 

   Vizupedia also has support for all major sharing platforms which can be accessed from any stage of network exploration so you can share a link directly to a node, connection, article, and more.

   You can choose to include data of your current network's connections to share the exact view of your Vizupedia at the time of sharing.

   When the recipient accesses the shared link, it will either bring them to the initial default of Vizupedia and animate to the contextual location of wherever the link is addressed, or the recipient will see an animation of the last 3 steps that the sender performed before sharing depending on if connection data was chosen to be included.
